Hello, I am Jac!

I am a Product Manager at BuzzFeed.

Previously, I was the Design Lead at BuzzFeed creating analytic tools for 500+ editorial and business users. Before that, I worked as a design consultant for French news media organizations in Paris & Bordeaux building online news platforms from ground-up. As an art director, I led a small design team at Gamelab shipping 11 causal game titles for the mass audiences.

I also teach at the NYU Game Design program.
