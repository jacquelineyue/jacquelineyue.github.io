Hello! I am Jac.

I design product.

With over a decade of design experience and leadership, I work with small business to multinational organizations to design engaging, actionable products for news media, games and data analystic.

Currently at BuzzFeed, I lead the design and product problems for a suite of internal analytic tools that empowers 500+ employees with data for learning and experimentation. In the day-to-day, I design flow and ui details, run user research, manage products, analyze usage data, write codes, mentor designers and co-lead a cross-disciplined team.

I enjoy turning nebulous concepts into buildable solutions, and empowering tool users as well as my team to do their best work. While not working, I throw things and explore system design with my son.
