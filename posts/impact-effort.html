Impact ✕ Effort Framework

Create a simple framework to visual and measure project for prioritization

Sequencing the projects for a cross-disciplined team is a constant challenge. As part of team that build internal analytic tools at BuzzFeed, most projects have multiple dependencies, both among ourselves and with other teams. Some key challenges are:

  • We got many ideas. Which projects we should work on first?
  • How can we prioritize projects that are the most impactful to our users?
  • We are low on engineering staffs. Is there tasks that requires no engineering to move the project forward?
  • How long does this tasks actually takes end-to-end?
  • Good cadence! What are the quick-wins projects we can bang out while chipping away a big investment project, so the users feel the impact of the incremental improvements and our team feels the continuous impact of their works?

This is where Impact x Effort framework comes in

To help making an informative decision on prioritization, I create a simple framework that visualizes user impact and team effort by a simple scoring system. It is intended to quantify "why" and "how"" of each project, compare them against the same metrics, and help negotiate their priority.
