Hello, I am Jac!

I specialize in design direction & product strategy.

With over a decade of design experience and leadership, I help multinational organizations, in-house teams, and startups to design system and build impactful products for news, games, and data.

Currently at BuzzFeed, I design and build a suite of analytic tools that empower the editorial and business team for learning and experimentation along with a cross-disciplinary team. I manage and lead product strategy for content and audience insights tooling, to support the evolving needs of our users and business.

I am passionate about approaching problems holistically and creating viable solutions that empower people and drive long-lasting impact. While not working, I like to throw things, fiddle some codes and explore map design with my son.
