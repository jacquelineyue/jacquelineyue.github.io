Jacqueline Yue

Design direction & product strategy

With over a decade of design experience and leadership, I help multinational companies, in-house teams, and startups to build impactful products for news, games, and data.

Currently at BuzzFeed, I lead design a suite of analytic tools that empower 600+ internal users to make data-driven decision. I also lead the product strategy and the cross-disciplined team to build our first audience insights dashboard as well as grow our existing content insights tools.

Previously, I worked as a design director consultant for French media companies – France Télévisions, Groupe Sud Ouest & Le Monde, and design director at gamelab.

I am passionate about tackling complex information design, approaching problems systematically and creating viable solutions that empower people and drive long-lasting impact. While not working, I like to fiddle the code and explore diagram design with my son.
